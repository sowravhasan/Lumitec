Link Meetings to Customers without Sending Invitations or Reminders, while still keeping Client Information in Odoo

The problem faced by the sales representative was that when they opened their calendar and wanted to schedule a new meeting with a client, the system automatically included the client as attendee on the top, but sometimes they don’t want to sendout invitation to the client, but still keep the connection between meeting and the contact (res.partner) or lead(crm.lead).

Solution :

The solution has now been implemented, which includes a feature in both the contact and CRM systems that allows the meeting to be linked to the customer without automatically sending an email invitation. This keeps the information on the client, but don’t send out any kind of invitation or reminders.

One-Click Feature Saves Time and Simplifies Your Workflow

With just a click, you can access the feedback section and enter information quickly and efficiently, saving time and simplifying your workflow.
The problem that the sales representative faced was that when they wanted to add feedback about a meeting, they had to search for the relevant information first and it was time consuming.

Solution :

After installing the App, only with one-click you can open the feedback-section.
A small arrow has been added in the right corner of the user interface, which allows the sales representative to click on it and open a window where they can write down the feedback. This feature eliminates the need for the sales representative to manually search for the relevant information, streamlining the process and making it more efficient.

One-Click Feature to Address Navigation and Route Planning

With just a click you can find and navigating to client locations, without first search for the client's address and then manually enter it into the GPS device.
The problem faced by the sales representative was that when they needed to visit a client, they had to first search for the client's address and then manually enter it into their GPS device or mapping application. This process was time-consuming and often led to delays or errors, which could impact the success of the sales visit.

Solution :

After installing the App, only with one-click they are able to see the address of the client.
When the sales representative opens the meeting information for a client, they simply need to click on the "Navigate to" button, which would open a new window in Google Maps or another GPS mapping application. This new window would automatically populate the client's address, eliminating the need for the sales representative to manually enter it into their GPS. Once the address is populated, the sales representative can easily click on the "route planner" feature, which would provide them with turn-by-turn directions to the client's location. This streamlined process saved the sales representative time and reduced the risk of errors or delays.
